Farinata

Chickpea flat bread is soooo goooood.

Ingredients

  • water
  • chickpea flour
  • sea salt
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • yellow onion
  • fresh herbs
  • black pepper

Instructions

  1. whisk together water, chickpea flour, salt, and olive oil
  2. let sit for at least one hour
  3. sautee onion
  4. stir onions and herbs into the flour mixture
  5. heat oven to 400
  6. cook in oven safe skillet for 20 to 30 minutes
  7. poke it with a fork, yea
  8. now enjoy!
