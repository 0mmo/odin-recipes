Roasted Kale

Kale is very good for you.

Ingredients

  • kale
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • fleur de Sel

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. cut out stems, tear large leaves in half, wash and dry
  3. sprinkle with salt and pepper
  4. roast for 15 minutes in single layers, and then sprinkle with fleur de solz
