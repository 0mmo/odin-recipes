Anchoiade

Anchoiade is amazinggg.

Ingredients:

  • anchovy fillets
  • garlic cloves, minced
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • tablespoons finely chopped fresh leaf parsley
  • freshly ground pepper or red chili flakes
  1. coarsley chop anchovies, combine with minced garlic and olive oil in pan over heat set to medium low
  2. once anchovies have disolved remove from heat and combine with remaining ingredients, serve warm
